Vincent Fung

Data Analyst   |   Dashboard Developer

As a creative and detail-driven data analyst / dashboard developer, I specialize in creating dashboards in Power BI, Qlik Sense, Qlikview and Tableau.

Before starting my journey in data analytics, I worked in digital marketing for over six years where I was able to hone and develop my technical skills. HTML, CSS and responsive web design are just some of the skills I've picked up along the way.

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed solving puzzles. So that’s how I look at data analytics; it is a lot like putting together a jigsaw puzzle. The first task is to collect all the puzzle pieces, then fitting them together to get a better understanding of the bigger picture. I take pride in helping organizations to convert raw data into meaningful insights that can then be used to make smarter and more informed business decisions.

Interests

In my spare time, I enjoy being with my family and friends, playing basketball, hiking, traveling and sightseeing (pre-pandemic), as well as practicing photography.

I purchased my first DSLR camera in 2012, shortly after my first child was born, when I realized that my point-and-shoot just wasn't going to cut it anymore. It opened up a whole new world of creativity for me and since then I have developed an avid interest in photography. My desire to capture aesthetically pleasing images of my daughter drove me to learn the art of photography and post-processing. Over the years, my interest in photography grew deeper. My favourite photography subjects are nature, landscapes, and my kids. Photography not only allows me to recognize the beauty in the details, but it also inspires me to use my creative mind to think and work “outside the box".


Sunset in Osaka


Sunset approaches over the Dōtonbori canal, in downtown Osaka, Japan. The first hour of light just after dawn and the last hour of light just before sunset is often called "the golden hour" in photography. It is my favourite time to shoot, as the sunlight bathes the scene with wonderful tones that make everything warmer, more beautiful, and more dramatic.
