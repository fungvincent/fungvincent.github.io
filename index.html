Business Intelligence Analyst   |   Turning Data into Insights

I’m a Business Intelligence Analyst focused on turning complex data into actionable insights. I build intuitive dashboards using Power BI, Qlik Sense, QlikView, and Tableau to help organizations better understand performance and make confident, data-driven decisions.

Before transitioning into analytics, I worked in advertising operations and campaign management, where I managed digital campaigns end to end. That experience shapes how I approach data today—grounding insights in real business context, from user behavior to growth opportunities.

I bring a problem-solving mindset to my work, focusing on simplifying complexity and uncovering the story behind the numbers. The goal is to deliver insights that are clear, practical, and drive meaningful outcomes.

Sunset in Osaka


Sunset approaches over the Dōtonbori canal, in downtown Osaka, Japan. The first hour of light just after dawn and the last hour of light just before sunset is often called "the golden hour" in photography. It is my favourite time to shoot, as the sunlight bathes the scene with wonderful tones that make everything warmer, more beautiful, and more dramatic.
