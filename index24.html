Vincent Fung

Data Analyst   |   Dashboard Developer

As a creative and detail-driven data analyst / dashboard developer, I specialize in creating dashboards in Power BI, Qlik Sense, Qlikview and Tableau.

Before starting my journey in data analytics, I worked in digital marketing for over six years where I was able to hone and develop my technical skills. HTML, CSS and responsive web design are just some of the skills I've picked up along the way.

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed solving puzzles. So that’s how I look at data analytics; it is a lot like putting together a jigsaw puzzle. The first task is to collect all the puzzle pieces, then fitting them together to get a better understanding of the bigger picture. I take pride in helping organizations to convert raw data into meaningful insights that can then be used to make smarter and more informed business decisions
